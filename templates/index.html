Own Investment Committee

COMPANY nas an investment committee which consists of 12 people. The Investment Committee is responsible for ensuring the management of the Company's portfolio in accordance with its investment objective and policy. Approves the strategy and transactions of the Company. The Investment Committee has significant investment experience in the UK and continental Europe, so you can apply a wide context and apply a consistent and disciplined investment approach in all funds.

Unique investment policy

The uniqueness lies in owning a diversified portfolio of real estate with good fundamental characteristics, as described below. The company invests mainly in the sectors of commercial real estate, including office, retail and industrial, and also invests in other sectors, including mixed use, residential real estate, hotels, healthcare and leisure. A separate area of investment is the purchase of residential real estate and hotels and their rental.

Diversification and Asset Allocation

The Board of Directors believes that in order to maximize the stability of the Company's revenues, the best strategy is to invest in a portfolio of assets diversified by location, sector, asset size and exposure to tenants with low vacancy rates and creditable tenants. The value of any individual asset at the time of its acquisition should not exceed 15% of gross assets, and the share of rental income received from one tenant should not exceed 10%.

Interest rate

The policy of the Board of Directors is to hedge interest rate risk either by securing fixed rate borrowing or by using interest rate swaps / derivatives used exclusively for hedging purposes
